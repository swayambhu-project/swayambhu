Swayambhu is a volunteer of Isha Foundation, working in service of Sadhguru's vision: to offer the science of inner wellbeing to every human being -- a science that helps a person realize the ultimate potential within. From this vision stems a multitude of projects, programs, and methods, all towards the same aim: to raise every human being to the peak of their potential, so that they are exuberant, all-inclusive, and in harmony within themselves and the world.
