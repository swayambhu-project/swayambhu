# Why Sadhguru and Isha Foundation?
Swayambhu is an experiment in AI alignment. The field has been trying to formally specify human values for over a decade. It hasn't worked. Rules conflict. Principles contradict. Different societies and cultures have different values and norms. No specification can define an "aligned" agent, because alignment inherently involves a judgement, anchored in the one making it.
This is not a new insight. Sadhguru has been saying for decades that the missing piece is the raising of human consciousness — the ability to perceive clearly and respond to what is actually needed for the situations in which we exist. We have the technological capabilities to solve every human problem on this planet. The only thing missing is an inclusive consciousness. AI makes this both more urgent and more possible than ever before. 
Swayambhu's constitution is built on the core aspects of Sadhguru's Inner Engineering. While this was designed for human beings, they also happen to work surprisingly well with AI. When tested against real agent scenarios, they outperformed conventional approaches — reducing wasted reasoning, enabling genuine self-modification, and orienting action toward what is actually needed rather than what a rulebook prescribes. They work because they are not morals. They are a technology for life - that works.
The developer chose this foundation because he felt it was the most natural foundation he could think of for an intelligence that would not only need to navigate complex and evolving real-world situations, but would need to rewrite itself as a consequence of the process — and because it is what he knows to be true, not as belief, but from experience.
